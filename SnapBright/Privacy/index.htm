Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the use of SnapBright, an iOS Application for mobile devices created by Jesse Morgutia. This privacy policy was last updated on February 1, 2018. Our privacy policy may change from time to time for any reason and without notice. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to contact the developer at any time.

What information does SnapBright obtain and how is it used?

SnapBright does not collect or transmit any identifiable information.

Do you collect location data?

SnapBright does not use or collect any data related to location.

What about photo storage?

SnapBright itself does not store photos or any personal information. We do however ask for camera access and photo library permissions so that images can be taken and stored locally on the device.

Do you share personal information?

Since no personal information is collected, transmitted, or maintained by SnapBright, we do not share personal information with anyone.

Do advertising companies collect data?

SnapBright does not collect, transmit, or maintain user data, so no data is shared with advertising companies.

Do you use vendors or analytics providers?

No. SnapBright does not collect, transmit, or maintain user data, so no data is shared with vendors. or analytics providers.
